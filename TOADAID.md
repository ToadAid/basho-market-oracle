# 🛡️ ToadAid: The AI Trading System for a Lasting World

This repository is more than just a trading bot. It is an **Open Source Educational Hub** designed to demonstrate how artificial intelligence can be used to guard capital, cultivate wisdom, and find stillness in a noisy market.

Inspired by the [ToadAid Mission](https://toadaid.github.io), this system follows three core pillars:

## 1. Stillness (The Clarity Filter)
In a world of hype and 24/7 noise, the **Researcher Agent** acts as a filter. It uses live RSS feeds, social sentiment, and institutional technical indicators to find high-signal opportunities. It prioritizes long-term endurance over rapid expansion.

## 2. Wisdom (The Perpetual Ledger)
The **Wisdom Ledger** ensures the agent never forgets a lesson. Every trade, success or failure, is recorded and analyzed. These lessons become "Commandments"—immutable rules that the AI must follow in all future sessions. This is our "Open Infrastructure" for long-term financial survival.

## 3. Multi-Brain Consensus (The Council)
To prevent the bias of a single mind, we use a **Council of Models**. Specialized agents (Researcher, Executor, Risk Manager) collaborate and critique each other's work. No trade proposal reaches the user without a second opinion from the Risk Manager.

---

## 🎓 Educational Mission
This project exists to empower the community. Through the **Agent Tutor** role, users can learn:
- How professional technical indicators (SuperTrend, ADX, RSI) actually work.
- How to architect a trade plan with Fibonacci retracements and ATR stops.
- How to perform security audits on smart contracts to avoid scams.

## ❤️ About ToadAid
ToadAid is a self-funded mission dedicated to providing:
- **Education Aids**: Empowering people with the tools to navigate the Web3 world safely.
- **Web3 Project Grants**: Supporting builders who align with our values of stillness and clarity.
- **Healthcare Aids**: Providing direct relief to those in need.

By open-sourcing this elite trading system, we hope to build a more resilient community of lore-keepers who can manage their own capital with the same care and wisdom that ToadAid brings to the world.

*Guarding the Lore. Cultivating the Wisdom.*
